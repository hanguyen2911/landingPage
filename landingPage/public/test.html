About Sunrise

Our charity organization was founded in 1994 based on the acute need to raise money for disadvantaged and at-risk children.

Sunrise steps in where health care ends, providing direct help to children with special needs. For over 30 years, we have ensured that children have the support to reach their potential and thrive. Since our foundation, we have distributed more than $30 million in funding to families and organizations in communities.
